  Here it is. The first Alpine production: A 64k-Intro for Wired '97.
  First of all, we're sorry for releasing such a low-quality-intro. We would
  have liked to release a better one as our first, but we lacked extremely
  of time. In fact the essence of an intro, i.e the effects, were just
  thrown together in a few days.
  Most of the time was spent on our 3D-Engine and our Music-system.
  Unfortunately you can't see much of the power hidden in the
  3D-Engine ( Just this gouraud-shaded Logo... )
  As to the Player: It is a S3M-Player with real panning support ( which was
  neither used in this intro ) It supports SB/SBPro/SB16-Cards ( AFAIK it was
  the only intro using the SB16 of the competition-compi ) but unfortunately
  no GUS ( as you might have noticed, so if you want to support us... ;)
